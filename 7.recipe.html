Sugar Free Chai Recipe Chai Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • 2-3 fresh gingers
  • 2-3 cardamom(elaichi)

Instructions

  • Boil 2 cups of water
  • Add gingers, cardamom and black tea leaves
  • Add milk
  • Let it boil for some time
  • Serve
